Last Updated: 13 May 2025

This privacy policy applies to mobile applications developed by Grindset Studios (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") and offered as free services. These applications are intended for use "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

The applications collect information when you download and use them. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address (e.g., IP address)
  • The pages of the applications that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
  • The time spent on the applications
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device
  • Geolocation Information: Approximate location used for certain in-app features, recommendations, and advertisements
  • Device Identifiers: Including device ID (e.g., IDFA, AAID), MAC address, and other identifiers
  • Crash logs and performance data: For improving app functionality and fixing bugs
  • Purchase history: If applicable, for processing in-app purchases

The applications do not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device unless specifically required for features like location-based services or advertisements.

The Service Provider may use the information you provide to contact you from time to time with important updates, required notices, or marketing promotions.

For a better experience, while using the applications, you may be asked to provide personally identifiable information. This information will be retained and used as described in this privacy policy.

These third-party partners may collect and process information such as:

  • Your device's IP address

We may share aggregated, anonymized data with these third parties for analytics, marketing, and improving user experience.

Please note that the applications utilize third-party services that have their own privacy policies. Below are links to the privacy policies of these third-party service providers:

3. Cookies and Other Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., device identifiers) to collect data about your interactions with our applications. This helps us improve your experience and provide more relevant ads.

We may collect the following types of data through these technologies:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, AAID)
  • Operating system version and device model
  • Platform type (Apple iOS, Android)
  • Browser type and language
  • User interactions with in-app features
  • Pages viewed and the time spent on them

These technologies are used for:

  • Personalizing our Services and improving user experience
  • Delivering targeted ads (behavioral advertising)
  • Analytics and performance improvement
  • Security and fraud prevention

If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your device, you can disable them through your device settings. However, some features of the application may not function correctly without cookies.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data

  • Restrict the processing of your personal data, in specific situations
  • Object to the processing of your personal data (including for marketing)
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time
  • Data portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
